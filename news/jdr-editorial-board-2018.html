A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of the Journal of Dental Research

The Journal of Dental Research is the flagship peer-reviewed publication of the International Association for Dental, Oral and Craniofacial Research — one of the most cited journals in dentistry.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa joined the Editorial Board of the Journal of Dental Research (JDR) in 2018. Published by SAGE on behalf of the International Association for Dental, Oral and Craniofacial Research (IADR), the JDR is one of the most prestigious and widely cited peer-reviewed journals in dentistry and oral sciences.

Covering oral biology, dental materials, tissue engineering, implantology, caries research and clinical sciences, the JDR prioritises original research with clear scientific merit and translational potential. It consistently ranks among the highest-impact publications in dentistry.

“

The JDR bridges laboratory discovery and clinical translation across dental sciences.

As an Editorial Board Member, A/P Rosa contributes his expertise in dental materials, graphene nanotechnology and dental pulp tissue engineering to the peer-review process, supporting the journal's commitment to rigorous, interdisciplinary evaluation at the intersection of materials science, regenerative dentistry and biocompatibility research.
