A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of the European Journal of Oral Sciences

The European Journal of Oral Sciences is one of Europe's leading multidisciplinary journals covering biological, clinical and social aspects of oral health.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa joined the Editorial Board of the European Journal of Oral Sciences (EJOS) in 2024. Published by Wiley, EJOS is one of Europe's leading multidisciplinary journals covering biological, clinical and social aspects of oral health, with an international readership spanning research and clinical practice.

The journal publishes original research in oral biology, dental materials, craniofacial biology, periodontology, oral microbiology and dental public health. Its broad scope and commitment to cross-disciplinary science position it at the intersection of oral biology, medicine and bioengineering.

EJOS covers the full breadth of oral sciences — from cellular biology to clinical outcomes.
