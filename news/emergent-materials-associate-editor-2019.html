A/P Rosa appointed Associate Editor of Emergent Materials

Emergent Materials is a Springer Nature journal dedicated to advanced materials with emergent properties across biology, chemistry and engineering.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa was appointed as Associate Editor of Emergent Materials in 2019. Published by Springer Nature, the journal covers the development and application of advanced materials with emergent properties — spanning biomedical systems, nanotechnology, energy and environmental science.

Emergent Materials bridges disciplinary boundaries by publishing work on functional materials with applications across biology, chemistry and engineering. The journal emphasises research on materials that demonstrate novel properties arising from their structure, composition or interactions with biological systems.

Emergent Materials publishes research at the frontier of advanced materials and life sciences.

As Associate Editor, A/P Rosa oversees peer review of manuscripts related to biomedical materials, nanomaterials and surface science — areas directly aligned with his research on graphene nanocoatings, dental biomaterials and bioinspired functional surfaces at NUS.
