A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of Dental Materials

Dental Materials is the flagship journal of the Academy of Dental Materials and one of the most influential publications in dental biomaterials science.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa joined the Editorial Board of Dental Materials in 2020. Published by Elsevier on behalf of the Academy of Dental Materials (ADM), Dental Materials is the flagship journal of the ADM and one of the most influential publications in the field of dental biomaterials science.

The journal covers the full spectrum of dental materials research — from materials characterisation, biocompatibility and mechanical testing to tissue engineering, 3D printing and smart material systems. It prioritises original research with clear relevance to the development and clinical translation of next-generation dental materials.

Dental Materials is the primary publication for the global dental biomaterials community.

A/P Rosa's contributions to the Editorial Board reflect his broad expertise across dental materials research — including calcium silicate cements, graphene-based materials, biocompatibility testing and regenerative biomaterials. His work aligns closely with the journal's mission to advance the science of materials for oral health.
