A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of JADA Foundational Science

JADA Foundational Science is the companion peer-reviewed journal of the Journal of the American Dental Association, bridging basic science and clinical dental practice.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa was invited to join the Editorial Board of JADA Foundational Science in 2020. JADA Foundational Science is the companion peer-reviewed journal of the Journal of the American Dental Association (JADA), published by Elsevier on behalf of the American Dental Association (ADA).

The journal focuses on the scientific foundations of clinical dentistry — covering dental materials, oral biology, biomechanics, pharmacology, microbiology and translational research. It bridges the gap between laboratory discovery and clinical application, serving as a forum for research that shapes evidence-based dental practice.
