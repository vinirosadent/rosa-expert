A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of the Journal of Clinical Periodontology

The Journal of Clinical Periodontology is the flagship publication of the European Federation of Periodontology — one of the most influential peer-reviewed journals in periodontology and implant dentistry.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa joined the Editorial Board of the Journal of Clinical Periodontology (JCP) in 2026. Published by Wiley on behalf of the European Federation of Periodontology (EFP), JCP is one of the most influential peer-reviewed journals in periodontology and implant dentistry, read by clinicians and researchers worldwide.

The journal covers clinical periodontology, periodontal biology, periodontal-systemic disease links, implant therapy and regenerative periodontal treatment. It publishes clinical trials, systematic reviews and laboratory studies.
