A/P Rosa appointed Associate Editor of the Journal of Prosthodontics

The Journal of Prosthodontics is the official publication of the American College of Prosthodontists — the leading peer-reviewed journal in prosthodontic science and practice.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa was appointed as Associate Editor of the Journal of Prosthodontics in 2020. Published by Wiley on behalf of the American College of Prosthodontists (ACP), the Journal of Prosthodontics is the official publication of the ACP and the leading peer-reviewed resource dedicated to all aspects of prosthodontic science and practice.

The journal covers the design, fabrication and evaluation of dental prostheses, occlusal rehabilitation, implant prosthodontics, maxillofacial prosthetics and removable and fixed prosthodontics. It prioritises research that advances evidence-based standards for restorative and prosthetic dentistry.
