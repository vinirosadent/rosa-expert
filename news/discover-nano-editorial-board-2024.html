A/P Rosa joins the Editorial Board of Discover Nano

Discover Nano is a Springer Nature open-access journal covering the full breadth of nanoscience and nanotechnology — from fundamental materials synthesis to biomedical and environmental applications.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa was invited to join the Editorial Board of Discover Nano in 2024. Published by Springer Nature, Discover Nano is an open-access journal covering nanoscience and nanotechnology across disciplines, including nanoparticles, nanocomposites, carbon nanomaterials and their integration into biomedical devices, sensors and functional coatings.

The journal welcomes research on the design, characterisation and application of nanomaterials across biology, medicine, engineering and environmental science. Its open-access model extends its reach to a global readership.

A/P Rosa's appointment is grounded in his extensive work on graphene-based nanomaterials for dental and biomedical applications — including graphene nanocoatings for implants, graphene substrates for cell differentiation and the development of nanocomposites for dental biomaterials.
