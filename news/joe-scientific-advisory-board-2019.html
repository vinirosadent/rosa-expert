A/P Rosa joins the Scientific Advisory Board of the Journal of Endodontics

The Journal of Endodontics is the flagship publication of the American Association of Endodontists — the leading clinical and scientific reference in endodontic research.

Associate Professor Vinicius Rosa was appointed to the Scientific Advisory Board of the Journal of Endodontics in 2019. Published by Elsevier on behalf of the American Association of Endodontists (AAE), the Journal of Endodontics is the leading peer-reviewed publication covering all aspects of endodontic science and practice.

The journal covers endodontic biology, vital pulp therapy, regenerative endodontics, root canal treatment, dental traumatology and new instrumentation technologies. It serves as the primary reference for both practicing endodontists and research scientists worldwide.
